Translation (mRNA to tRNA)

RNA, or ribonucleic acid, is a molecule essential for life that helps translate genetic information from DNA into proteins.

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RNA

tRNA, or Transfer RNA are molecules bring specific amino acids to the ribosome. Each tRNA has an anticodon, a three-nucleotide sequence that matches a specific mRNA codon.
